## Decisions

### APPROVED — Name legal reminder preset groups on deadline calculators

**User-impact note:** Target users are Swiss construction owners, contractors, and project admins selecting calendar reminder offsets for legal deadline exports/share links. The workflow moment is choosing which reminders will accompany a Ruegefrist or dashboard deadline calculation. This is not just incremental polish: these controls affect legal deadline reminder artifacts, and assistive-technology users need the reminder context to choose offsets confidently.

**Engineering task:** In `components/ruegefrist-calculator.tsx` and `app/dashboard/deadlines/page.tsx`, add stable labels and `role="group"` / `aria-labelledby` around the existing reminder preset buttons. Add regression assertions in `__tests__/ruegefrist-calculator.test.tsx` and `__tests__/deadlines-share-link.test.tsx` that the groups are queryable by the localized reminder label.

### BACKLOG — Deadline reminder summary next to exported `.ics` actions

Valuable Medium follow-up that would make export/share review clearer, but it needs localized copy/layout on two surfaces and is not necessary before the accessibility naming contract is in place.

### BACKLOG — Unified deadline-to-action cockpit across calculators, cases, and protocol finalization

Strategically strongest, but too large and currently high-overlap with open cases PRs #148 and #149. Future phase-1 should wait for those branches to settle, then build a confirmed timeline/view-model helper or read-only dashboard card.
