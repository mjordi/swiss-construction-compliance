## Decisions

### APPROVED — Show selected reminder offsets next to deadline export/share actions

**User-impact note:** Target users are Swiss construction owners, contractors, and project admins exporting or sharing legal deadline reminder artifacts. The workflow moment is after calculation, just before copying a link or downloading an `.ics` calendar file. This is not just incremental polish: reminder offsets become part of legal calendar/share artifacts, and users should be able to verify them at the exact action point.

**Engineering task:** In `components/ruegefrist-calculator.tsx` and `app/dashboard/deadlines/page.tsx`, render a compact selected-reminder summary near the share/download action area once results exist, including an explicit `none` state when no reminder offsets are selected. Add focused assertions in `__tests__/ruegefrist-calculator.test.tsx` and `__tests__/deadlines-share-link.test.tsx`.

### BACKLOG — Add a deadline reminder review card before `.ics` download

Valuable, but a richer review card would require additional layout/copy decisions across two surfaces. Today's approved summary provides the main trust signal with lower review risk.

### BACKLOG — Unified deadline-to-action cockpit across calculators, cases, and protocol finalization

Strategically strongest, but too broad and currently overlaps active cases PRs. Future phase-1 should start with a pure action/timeline derivation helper after #148/#149 settle.
