## Approved

### APPROVED — Add a first legal milestone timeline to case details

Target user: Swiss contractor, owner, or project administrator reviewing a defect dossier.

Workflow moment: before sending a notice, creating a linked protocol, or preparing an audit file, the user needs to verify the legal sequence from contract through discovery to the statutory deadline.

User impact: this is not incremental polish. It turns separate legal facts into an ordered, explainable chronology and delivers the first visible phase of the repeatedly backlogged audit/activity cockpit. A real user can scan the sequence instead of reconstructing it mentally.

Engineering task: add a pure legal-milestone derivation helper to `lib/case-timeline.ts`; render localized contract, defect-discovery, and applicable notice-deadline milestones in the expanded details of `app/dashboard/cases/page.tsx`; add translation keys in `locales/index.ts`; add focused helper and Cases UI regression tests. Keep it read-only and do not change deadline calculations, persistence, protocol queries, filters, exports, or routes.
