## Decisions

### APPROVED — Add a protocol final-review summary before finalization

**User-impact note:** Target users are Swiss construction owners, contractors, and admins finalizing a legally relevant acceptance protocol from a linked case or standalone workflow. The workflow moment is immediately before pressing Finalize Protocol. This is not just incremental polish: protocol finalization persists legal evidence and can sync a linked case checklist, so users should see a compact review of project, case/deadline, defect/no-defect capture, and signature state before committing.

**Engineering task:** In `app/dashboard/page.tsx`, render a compact final-review card on step 2 using existing state: project name, linked-case status/deadline or standalone mode, defect/no-defect capture state, and signature readiness. Add localized copy in `locales/index.ts` and focused coverage in `__tests__/dashboard-linked-case-retry.test.tsx`.

### BACKLOG — Add finalization-ready checklist copy to protocol step 2

Useful but weaker than the approved item because it only explains blockers. Fold the spirit of this into the richer final-review card rather than shipping a separate micro-patch.

### BACKLOG — Unified compliance activity cockpit across cases, vault, protocols, and reminders

Strategically strongest, but too large for one daily PR. The smallest credible future phase-1 is a read-only activity card after existing protocol/vault/case summaries are consistently explicit.
