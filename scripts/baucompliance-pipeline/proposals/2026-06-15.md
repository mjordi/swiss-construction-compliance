## Small (S) — Lock vault project-card navigation while archive/restore is pending

- **Problem:** When a vault archive/restore mutation is in flight, the archive button is disabled but the sibling full-card “open in cases” link remains active. Users can navigate away while the project is mid-transition, creating a stale or confusing handoff state.
- **Scope:** Mark the card link as disabled during that project’s pending status mutation, prevent click/keyboard navigation for that card only, and add focused regression coverage.
- **Expected impact:** Improves dashboard reliability during project organization actions and aligns the vault flow with existing pending-mutation lockout discipline.
- **Risk (1-10):** 2
- **Effort/size:** Small

## Medium (M) — Add accessible, project-specific archive/restore action names in the vault

- **Risk (1-10):** 4
- **Effort/size:** Medium
