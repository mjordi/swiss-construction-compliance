- **Problem:** Users can calculate a public 60-day Rügefrist scenario, but they cannot copy a link that restores the contract/discovery dates and result. Reopening or sharing the tool loses the scenario context.

- **Expected impact:** Makes the public legal calculator easier to share with clients/colleagues and aligns it with the dashboard deadlines share-link pattern.
- **Risk:** 3/10
- **Effort/size:** Small
