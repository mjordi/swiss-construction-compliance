# Daily Product Improvement Proposals — 2026-07-20

## User-and-market lens

Primary users are Swiss construction owners, contractors, and project admins who triage defect cases under the 2026 OR/SIA legal workflows. The workflow moment is the scan of the Cases ledger before deciding whether a defect case is ready for a traceable notice, has enough proof, and is protected by reminders/protocol evidence. A best-in-market Swiss construction-compliance product should make legal readiness obvious at card-scan level, not force users to open details or mentally combine checklist state, deadlines, protocols, and vault evidence.

Recent pipeline artifacts over the last 14 days show a strong pattern of S-sized, user-facing slices around reminders, protocol links, Vault audit snapshots, case scan-level signals, and checklist normalization. The recurring strategic direction is a unified audit/activity cockpit. The strongest near-term opportunity is still to complete missing scan-level readiness dimensions before attempting a broader timeline model.

## Proposal S — Show notice draft readiness in the case action snapshot

- **Target user and JTBD:** Swiss construction owner/project admin scanning defect cases to decide whether a legally relevant notice package is ready to send.
- **Problem:** The scan-level action snapshot shows deadlines, evidence, protocols, and reminders, but notice draft readiness is still hidden in the expanded checklist. Users can see that proof exists while missing that the actual notice text/package is not yet drafted.
- **Scope:** Add a localized read-only `Notice readiness` cell to the existing `cases-action-snapshot-*` grid, derived from the normalized `noticeDrafted` checklist key. Add focused RTL coverage.
- **Expected impact:** Improves legal triage confidence and completes another core case-readiness signal with a small, low-risk UI slice.
- **User-visible outcome:** Case cards show whether the notice draft is ready or pending before users open details.
- **Competitive / best-product rationale:** A best-in-market compliance product should surface proof + notice + reminder readiness together where legal action decisions happen.
- **Risk:** 2/10.
- **Effort/size:** S.
- **Phase-1 slice:** This proposal is already the phase-1 slice.

## Proposal M — Add a first read-only case audit summary row

- **Target user and JTBD:** Project admins reviewing active cases and wanting a concise audit cockpit summary across deadlines, evidence, protocols, reminders, and notice readiness.
- **Problem:** Scan-level readiness is split into several cells without an overall “audit ready / missing X” interpretation.
- **Scope:** Add a derived read-only summary row for one Cases surface that names the strongest missing legal-readiness prerequisite.
- **Expected impact:** Higher cognitive-load reduction than another single cell, because it starts turning raw signals into decision support.
- **User-visible outcome:** A case card highlights the next audit-readiness gap without opening details.
- **Competitive / best-product rationale:** Market-leading workflow tools explain what is blocking legal readiness, not only list statuses.
- **Risk:** 5/10.
- **Effort/size:** M.
- **Phase-1 slice:** Define and render the summary for the Cases ledger only, using existing normalized checklist/protocol/deadline data.

## Proposal L — Build a unified audit-ready activity timeline

- **Target user and JTBD:** Owners/contractors needing a chronological, defensible trail of defects, protocols, evidence, reminders, notices, and archive actions.
- **Problem:** Readiness signals exist across Cases, Vault, deadlines, and protocols, but there is no unified audit trail that answers “what happened, what is missing, and what must happen next.”
- **Scope:** Introduce a shared activity model and render it across dashboard surfaces.
- **Expected impact:** Step-change product differentiation and legal confidence.
- **User-visible outcome:** Users can review an audit-ready timeline rather than navigating separate surfaces.
- **Competitive / best-product rationale:** A unified legal activity cockpit is a strategic differentiator for Swiss construction compliance.
- **Risk:** 8/10.
- **Effort/size:** L.
- **Phase-1 slice:** Start with a read-only derived timeline helper for a single case after scan-level readiness signals are complete.
