## Proposal M — Show missing audit blockers on Vault project cards

- **Target user and JTBD:** Contractor/owner scanning the Tech Vault to decide whether a project dossier is legally ready before sending notices or archiving evidence.
- **Problem:** Vault cards show percentage and next legal action, but not the concrete missing checklist/protocol blockers. Users must open Cases to learn why a dossier is incomplete.
- **Scope:** Reuse existing case timeline/checklist/protocol state to compute a compact read-only missing-item summary on each Vault project card; add focused card-level regression coverage. Do not change persistence, routing, deadline math, or archive behavior.
- **Expected impact:** Users can triage evidence projects directly from the Vault and understand the next preparation gap immediately.
- **User-visible outcome:** Vault audit snapshot shows `Fehlt: …` for missing defect/evidence/notice/calendar/protocol items, or the existing complete state when all audit blockers are ready.
- **Competitive / best-product rationale:** A best-in-market compliance vault should be explainable, not just score-based; legal audit readiness should name the exact missing artifact.
- **Risk:** 3/10.
- **Effort/size:** M, bounded to one component/test file.
- **Phase-1 slice:** Add a read-only missing-blocker line to the existing Vault card audit snapshot.

## Proposal L — Build a unified audit activity timeline across Cases, Protocols, Vault, and Reminders

- **Target user and JTBD:** Compliance lead preparing a defensible defect file who needs a chronological legal artifact trail.
- **Problem:** Deadlines, protocols, evidence, reminders, and checklist state remain distributed across pages.
- **Scope:** Shared timeline model, cross-surface rendering, route handoffs, and export-ready artifact history.
- **Expected impact:** Major step toward an audit cockpit and legal defensibility.
- **User-visible outcome:** One chronological readiness/activity trail per construction defect case.
- **Competitive / best-product rationale:** This is the strategic north star for a compliance product: legal facts, tasks, evidence, and exports in one traceable timeline.
- **Risk:** 8/10.
- **Effort/size:** L.
- **Phase-1 slice:** Derive a read-only timeline helper for one case surface after Vault and Cases expose consistent audit-readiness explanations.
