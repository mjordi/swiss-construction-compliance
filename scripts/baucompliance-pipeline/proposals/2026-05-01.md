## Proposal S — Make finalize readiness explicit in the protocol wizard
- **Title**: Make finalize readiness explicit in the protocol wizard
- **Problem**: Users can currently click the finalize CTA with a signature but without defect capture confirmation, then only learn about the missing requirement after the attempt is blocked.
- **Scope**: Add a small finalize-readiness helper to the wizard, disable finalize until defect capture is satisfied, and cover the readiness logic with a focused unit test.
- **Expected impact**: Reduces avoidable failed finalize attempts and makes the protocol completion path clearer in a legally sensitive workflow.
- **Risk (1-10)**: 2
- **Effort/size**: Small

## Proposal M — Add linked-case quick jump from wizard legal context to Cases
- **Title**: Add linked-case quick jump from wizard legal context to Cases
- **Problem**: When a linked case is stale or urgent, the wizard surfaces legal context but gives no direct route to the case management view where evidence and checklist items can be completed.
- **Scope**: Add a contextual CTA from the wizard sidebar to the Cases page, pre-filtered to the linked case or triage state.
- **Expected impact**: Shortens recovery time for users who need to complete case follow-up before finalizing a protocol.
- **Risk (1-10)**: 4
- **Effort/size**: Medium

## Proposal L — Add cross-device autosaved protocol drafts backed by Supabase
- **Title**: Add cross-device autosaved protocol drafts backed by Supabase
- **Problem**: Draft recovery is still local-only, so users switching from field tablet to office desktop can lose in-progress protocol context.
- **Scope**: Persist wizard drafts server-side, hydrate across devices, resolve stale conflicts, and expose recovery/discard UX.
- **Expected impact**: Major improvement for team continuity and fewer lost protocol sessions.
- **Risk (1-10)**: 8
- **Effort/size**: Large
