# Daily Product Improvement Proposals — 2026-07-07

## User-and-market lens

Primary users are Swiss construction owners, contractors, and project teams using BauCompliance.ch to preserve defect-rights evidence, deadline awareness, and acceptance protocol records without legal ambiguity. The strongest workflow moment today is the handover-engine finalization step: after a user links a case, enters defect/no-defect evidence, and signs, they are about to create a legal PDF and persisted protocol. A best-in-market Swiss construction-compliance product should make the final legal context review obvious before finalization, especially when the protocol will sync back to a case checklist.

## PM insights

Recent pipeline work has strengthened case handoffs, vault readiness counts, reminder summaries, and missing linked-case warnings. The portfolio remains mostly S-sized but user-facing. The next safest step-up is not another hidden reliability patch: it is a bounded protocol finalization review summary that turns the existing linked-case context and readiness checks into an explicit final confirmation moment. There are no open PRs, and the recent merged cadence supports a focused S-to-bounded-M UI slice with tests.

## Proposal S — Add finalization-ready checklist copy to protocol step 2

- **Target user and job-to-be-done:** Site/project user preparing a handover protocol who needs to know whether defect evidence and signature are complete.
- **Problem:** Step 2 disables finalization until inputs are complete, but the user sees only individual warnings rather than a compact legal-readiness summary.
- **Scope:** Add a small readiness text block beside the finalize button explaining defect/signature completeness.
- **Expected impact:** Reduces confusion about why finalization is blocked.
- **User-visible outcome:** The user sees what remains before they can finalize.
- **Competitive / best-product rationale:** Professional compliance tools explain blockers, not just disable actions.
- **Risk:** 2/10.
- **Effort/size:** S.
- **Phase-1 slice:** N/A.

## Proposal M — Add a protocol final-review summary before finalization

- **Target user and job-to-be-done:** Swiss construction owners, contractors, and admins finalizing a legally relevant acceptance protocol from a linked case or standalone workflow.
- **Problem:** The product already stores linked case context and readiness state, but immediately before finalization users do not get one compact review of project, linked-case/deadline context, defect capture state, and signature state.
- **Scope:** In `app/dashboard/page.tsx`, render a compact review card on step 2 showing project name, linked-case status/deadline or standalone mode, defect/no-defect capture status, and signature status. Add localized copy and focused regression tests.
- **Expected impact:** Improves legal confidence at the exact moment a protocol is persisted/exported; reduces accidental standalone or incomplete-context finalizations.
- **User-visible outcome:** Users can verify the protocol context before pressing Finalize Protocol.
- **Competitive / best-product rationale:** Best-in-market legal workflow products provide an audit-ready “review before commit” moment, not only post-submit confirmation.
- **Risk:** 3/10.
- **Effort/size:** M, bounded to one card and tests.
- **Phase-1 slice:** Show a read-only final-review card using existing state; do not alter persistence, PDF schema, or deadline math.

## Proposal L — Unified compliance activity cockpit across cases, vault, protocols, and reminders

- **Target user and job-to-be-done:** Project managers overseeing multiple construction defects who need a single prioritized view of next legal actions.
- **Problem:** Cases, vault evidence, protocols, and reminders are improving but remain separate surfaces.
- **Scope:** A new dashboard cockpit that aggregates next actions, protocol counts, evidence readiness, reminders, and stale handoffs.
- **Expected impact:** Major activation and retention improvement through one operational command center.
- **User-visible outcome:** A single compliance queue showing what needs action next.
- **Competitive / best-product rationale:** Market-leading compliance software should provide an audit/action cockpit across artifacts.
- **Risk:** 8/10.
- **Effort/size:** L.
- **Phase-1 slice:** Backlog until more shared contracts are in place; a future phase-1 could add a read-only activity card from existing timeline/vault readiness data.
